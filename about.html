The Asian Eats Directory is a global directory of local culinary food wisdom. Inspired by people sharing Asian restaurants in effort to support local businesses, Athena and Jason decided to create a more permanent collection of the recommendations they've been seeing on Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, newspapers, and of course Reddit. Anything that can be a resource to local communities has been included: local lists, maps, and directories of Asian eateries for people to find great food and support local businesses.


While this project began to support East Asian businesses facing racism and tough times during Covid-19, we extend solidarity to all Asian communities. For us, Asia is broadly defined -- from Korea down to the tip of Indonesia, Japan, through the Middle East to the bank of the Bosphorus River in Istanbul.


All projects are linked to the original creators. Please click on the project and creator names to find delicious dishes from the Asian diaspora.


Contributors include Ashley Kang and annonymous contributors who helped with research and creating lists and maps. A big thank you to the people who are managing these directories for their communities!
