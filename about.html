The Asian Eats Directory is a global directory of local culinary food wisdom. Inspired by people sharing tips on where to order Asian food in this pandemic season, Athena and Jason created this collection based on posts and articles on social and printed media. Click on the projects and creator names to find delicious food from the Asian diaspora.
